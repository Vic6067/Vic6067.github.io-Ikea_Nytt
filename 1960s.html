The 1960s

Heading

From armchairs to cocktail cabinets, the 60s were a wild time!

Heading

Advertising was at it's most popular all over the world, and dynamic layouts became all the rage when it came to design. People were looking to do more with less, and storage compartments became a big thing that IKEA capitalized on. This was where IKEA really leaned into showcasing their product in room layouts instead of individually.
