From Nothing to Everything

The DIY furniture that changed homemaking forever.

The purpose of this site is to act as a design study on the progression of IKEA as a brand.
1950s

The iconic style of 50s post war design slowly meets homemaking.

1960s

This is where IKEA starts getting a more uniform look that is reminiscent of today.

1970s

More value on images and visually showcasing what IKEA is.

1980s

This is where IKEA really starts to experiment with colour and different overlays, while keeping to their original concept.

1990s

More experimentation with texture, you start to see IKEA move into more conceptual ways of conveying information.

2000s

The IKEA you know and love.
